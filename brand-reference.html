05 / Voice

Writing Rules

The brand voice is direct, specific, and unhedged. The same standards that apply to the tool outputs apply to everything written under the Lore name.

Do
Direct statements. No hedging, no qualifications.
Specific language. Name the problem, the cause, the fix.
Active voice. The tool does things; it does not facilitate them.
Short sentences when precision matters.
Do Not
Em dashes. Absolute prohibition. Replace with period, comma, or colon.
Parallel pair endings. "X is Y. Z is not." is a banned structure.
Parallel fragment triplets. "Built systems. Drove outcomes. Shaped culture."
Slop terms: seamlessly, leverage, throughline, convergence point, AI-era.
